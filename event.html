Dialogues

Numbers of dialogues will be organized between community people/public in the different places of Rural Chitwan. The main objectives of these events are to make interactive situation between public authorities and communities for the collaborative action by them to reduce the social problems existing in that society. By this situation issued are identified and develop the work plan together to reduce that problematic issue.

Chitwan

Empowering Futures:Chitwan Chapter

We will primarily work for children who had lost parents or other caregivers. The project will work with Orphanages to can fill a necessary gap in care and ultimately help the children find a suitable family. Even when a child does lose their parents, living with a foster family ensures a much better outcome for them than living in an orphanage. Indeed, this is why CAWRI is launching “Empowering Futures” in Chitwan.
