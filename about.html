Children And Women Rights International

CAWRI is a center for access to justice for women,children and all individuals facing violation of their rights on the basis of their identity. The organization runs with the mission to make available adequate protection and support service to them in situations of crisis, including provision of emergency shelter for women and children, legal aid and other necessary interventions to ensure their protection and rehabilitation. CAWRI also undertakes advocacy and training activities to improve mechanisms, practices and processes for their protection from violence, exploitation, abuse and neglect.
CAWRIâ€™s mission is to support women and children who are victims of violence or of denial of human rights and fundamental freedoms and to make available to them adequate protection and support services in situations of crisis. These services are directed towards facilitating their access to justice through provision of legal aid and other necessary interventions to ensure their protection, care and rehabilitation. The overriding concern of the organization is that protection and services to such women and children should be provided in a manner that recognizes and respects their right to liberty, dignity and safeguards their best interest. Its long term objective is to provide self-sustaining financial support to women and child.
Our vision is to foster a society where children and women are empowered to sustain themselves with dignity and resilience. We strive to transform external environments by gradually dismantling harmful social attitudes, cultural practices, and religious mindsets that perpetuate discrimination against women and obstruct community progress. Through advocacy and action, we aim to establish systems and institutions that enhance the implementation of laws and sustainable initiatives for child protection. By focusing on children in especially difficult circumstances, we work to spotlight their risks and vulnerabilities, galvanizing public support for state interventions and social initiatives that uphold and promote their rights. Our ultimate goal is to create a world where children and women thrive as agents of change in their communities, contributing to a more equitable and inclusive society.
